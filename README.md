# Public Record — VA Community Care Network Case

This repository documents an active False Claims Act case concerning billing manipulation within the US Department of Veterans Affairs Community Care Network, administered across 36 US states by Optum, a subsidiary of UnitedHealth Group.

The relator is Steven Barden, a 100% disabled US Air Force veteran. The case has been formally submitted to multiple federal channels including a sitting US Senator (Inquiry #2017816 active), the US Department of Justice National Fraud Enforcement Division, the FBI Tampa field office, the VA Office of Inspector General, and the Florida Attorney General. The case became public on April 23, 2026.

## What is in this repository

- The evidentiary document distributed to federal channels and press
- Redacted billing records corroborating the claims in that document
- Public correspondence relevant to the case
- Technical analysis of the billing infrastructure and the conditions under which the documented manipulation became possible
- Jurisdictional analysis of state and federal pathways
- Chronology of relevant events

## How to read this repository

If you are new to the case, start with `/01-public-statement/` and read the LinkedIn article. Then read the evidentiary PDF in `/02-evidentiary-pdf/`. Other folders provide supporting documentation.

## Document handling

Every document in this repository is one of three tiers:

- Original: the document as received from the source (provider, VA, insurer)
- OCR copy: the original processed through optical character recognition for searchability, with no content alteration
- Posted version: the OCR copy with specific redactions described in DISCLOSURE.md

Posted versions are what appear in this repository. Original and OCR copies are retained privately and can be produced on legitimate request through proper channels.
